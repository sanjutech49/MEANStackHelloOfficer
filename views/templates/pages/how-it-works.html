How It Works

By completing this traffic school, the point from your traffic citation will not show up on your driving record. This course is licensed by the DMV, completely online, and we file your certificate to the court for you electronically. All you need to do is read the curriculum and pass the final exam!

REGISTER

  1. Click the “Get Started” button.
  2. Fill out the short, basic information section.
  3. Answer a few security questions to ensure only you are taking the course.
  4. Create your user name and password.
  5. Log in and start!

READ THROUGH THE COURSE

Learn the important information with ease. Keep your eye out for some random sentences that are unrelated to traffic laws. They will show up on your open book final exam to make sure you didn’t just skip ahead.

ANSWER QUIZ QUESTIONS

Complete a quick three-question quiz at the end of each section. When you get the questions right, you can move on to the next section. You have an unlimited number of attempts to get the quiz questions right, so don’t worry!

After you pass each quiz, you will be asked to answer one random security question. If you get the security question wrong, you will be automatically logged out and will have to repeat the quiz when you sign back in.

PASS THE OPEN-BOOK FINAL EXAM

The test has 28 questions and is open-book. We make it easier than other traffic violator schools because we let you see the course curriculum and the test side-by-side!

You only need to get 70% to pass (that is 20 out of 28 questions correct). Don't worry if you fail the first one. You will be given another try. You can always take our course again if you fail twice.

COMPLETE DMV COURSE EVALUATION

Of course we appreciate your feedback, however all you have to do to fulfill this DMV requirement is to fill in your name, our name and license number.

GET YOUR COMPLETION RECEIPT

Hooray! You finished! And you’ve got a fancy receipt to prove it. Once you’ve printed/downloaded/emailed your receipt for your records, you are done! You don’t have to send anything! We will send the certificate to the court electronically.

Once we submit your certificate, it’s up to the court to handle the rest. Contact them directly to make sure they have resolved your ticket.
